
#### 012 Policy

cmake_policy(VERSION <min>[...<max>])

    it is being called when cmake_minimum_required running.

# set policy
cmake_policy(SET CMP<NNNN> NEW)
cmake_policy(SET CMP<NNNN> OLD) # not commemded

# get policy
cmake_policy(GET CMP<NNNN> <var>)

# policy stack
cmake_policy(PUSH)
cmake_policy(POP)
